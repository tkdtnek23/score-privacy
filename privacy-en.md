# Score Privacy Policy

Score (the "Service") values your personal information and strives to comply with applicable privacy laws. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how long we retain it, and your rights as a user.

## 1. Information We Collect

| Category | Items | How Collected |
|----------|-------|--------------|
| Account Information | Email address, social login identifier (Google/Kakao/Apple/Naver), Firebase UID | Automatically via social login |
| Profile Information | Nickname, profile image | Entered by user |
| Service Usage Data | Bowling scores, game records, frame data, handicap | Entered by user |
| Club & Community | Club info, member relationships, posts, events, notices, attached images | Entered by user |
| Device & Diagnostics | Device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, performance metrics | Automatically collected |

## 2. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used solely for the following purposes.

- Member registration and identity verification
- Providing core features such as bowling records, statistics, and rankings
- Club management and member administration
- Service quality improvement, incident response, and security
- Responding to violations of laws or terms of service

## 3. Retention and Deletion

- Personal information is deleted immediately upon account withdrawal.
- When required by law, information is retained for the specified period before deletion.

## 4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Score does not share personal information with third parties without user consent.

## 6. User Rights

Users may exercise the following rights at any time.

- View and modify personal information
- Request deletion of personal information
- Withdraw account (account deletion)
- Revoke consent

Rights may be exercised through the in-app settings menu or by contacting us at the address below. We will respond without delay.

## 7. Disposal of Personal Information

- Information is deleted immediately when it is no longer needed (e.g., upon account withdrawal or service termination).
- Electronic files are deleted using technical methods that prevent recovery or reconstruction.

## 8. Security Measures

- TLS encryption for data in transit
- Minimum access privileges and separated administrator accounts
- Firebase Realtime Database Security Rules
- Anomaly detection and access log monitoring

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service does not accept registrations from users under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect their information.
