# Score Support

For questions or bug reports while using the Score app, please reach out through the options below.

## Contact

- **Email**: [tkdtnek23@gmail.com](mailto:tkdtnek23@gmail.com)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I can't log in
- Check your network connection.
- Make sure the app is updated to the latest version.
- If the issue persists, please email us with your device model, iOS version, and the login method you used (Google / Kakao / Apple / LINE).

### My scores have disappeared
- Reconnect to the internet and relaunch the app — it will sync with the server automatically.
- If records are still missing, please email us with the club name and the date of the records.

### I want to delete my account
- Go to **Settings → Delete Account** in the app.
- If you have trouble, you can also request deletion via email.

### Other inquiries
- Feature requests, bug reports, and all other questions are welcome via email.

## Response Time

We aim to respond within 1–3 business days.
